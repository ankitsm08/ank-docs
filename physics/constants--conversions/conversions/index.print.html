Conversions

Length

$\begin{aligned} 1 \:m &= 39.37( \approx 243/8) \:in &&= 3.28( \approx 105/32) \:ft &&= 1.094( \approx 11/10) \:yd \\ 1 \:in &= 2.54( \approx 2/5) \:cm &&= 1/12 \:ft \\ 1 \:ft &= 12 \:in &&= 0.3048 \:m \\ 1 \:km &= 0.6214 \:mi &&= 3281 \:ft \\ 1 \:mi &= 5280 \:ft &&= 1.609 \:km \\ 1 \:\text{light-year} &= 9.461 \times 10^{12} \:km \end{aligned}$

Temperature

$\begin{aligned} \text{Kelvin, } & K &&= {}^\circ C + 273.15 \\ \text{Celsius, } & {}^\circ C &&= K - 273.15 &&= \dfrac{5}{9}({}^\circ F - 32) \\ \text{Fahrenheit, } & {}^\circ F &&= \dfrac{9}{5}{}^\circ C + 32 \\ \text{Rankine, } & {}^\circ R &&= {}^\circ F + 459.67&&= \dfrac{5}{9}K \end{aligned}$

Speed

$\begin{aligned} km/h &= \dfrac{5}{18} \:m/s , & m/s &= \dfrac{18}{5} \:km/h \\ mi/h &= 0.447 \:m/s , &ft/s &= 0.305 \:m/s \\ \end{aligned}$

Mass

$\begin{aligned} 1 \:kg &= 2.204 \:lb &&= 0.4536 \:oz \\ 1 \:lb &= 0.4536 \:kg &&= 16 \:oz \\ 1 \:oz &= 0.0283 \:kg \end{aligned}$
